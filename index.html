Global Coffee Market

This comprehensive project delves into the global coffee market trends from 2010 to 2020, driven by a personal connection to Ethiopia's rich coffee culture. Utilizing Python (Pandas) for data exploration and aggregation, Excel for data transformation, and Tableau for creating interactive dashboards, the study investigates key factors influencing coffee production. Key focus areas include the impact of agricultural land per capita across different income levels, regional specializations in coffee production, and the relationship between government investment in agricultural research and production efficiency. Data sources such as Our World in Data and the World Bank provided valuable insights. Despite challenges like data quality issues and the subjective nature of consumer preferences, the project successfully highlighted significant trends and produced an interactive Tableau dashboard to visualize the findings.
